🤖 AI & Intelligent Learning

Waddah

AI-powered virtual teaching assistant and large language model platform designed to provide employees with instant access to training knowledge, policies, procedures, and personalized learning guidance through natural language interactions.

TPLS (Training Passport & License System)

Blockchain-powered platform that maintains secure digital training records, certifications, and operational licenses while leveraging AI for compliance monitoring, license validation, competency tracking, and workforce readiness insights.

AI-Powered Ticketing System

Intelligent support platform capable of automatically categorizing, prioritizing, routing, and responding to employee inquiries while providing analytics and service performance insights.

🎓 Learning Experience & LMS

LMS Gamification Project

Enhancement of the learning experience through badges, points, leaderboards, challenges, and achievement tracking to improve learner engagement and course completion rates.

LMS Proctoring Tools

Implementation of advanced online assessment monitoring capabilities including identity verification, activity tracking, and AI-assisted proctoring to strengthen examination integrity.

TMS (ALOS)

Optimization and integration of the ALOS Training Management System to streamline training administration, scheduling, records management, and regulatory compliance.

🚀 Digital Transformation & Innovation

Simulator

First-of-its-kind simulation-based training environment fully integrated with the LMS, enabling employees to practice realistic ground handling operations, validate competencies, and track performance within a risk-free virtual environment.

LEAD Program Portfolio

Centralized portfolio management solution providing visibility into participants, projects, achievements, learning pathways, and performance metrics across the LEAD Program.

Policy Manual Website

Centralized digital platform enabling employees to easily access, search, review, and acknowledge organizational policies, procedures, and operational manuals.

RFID/Barcode for Hajj Baggage

Real-time baggage tracking solution utilizing RFID and barcode technologies to improve baggage visibility, handling accuracy, operational efficiency, and passenger experience during Hajj operations.
